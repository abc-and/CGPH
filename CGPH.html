A youth-led movement advocating for environmental sustainability in the Philippines.

Founded in April 2022, Code Green PH is committed to promoting solidarity for conservation and climate action. Through mass information campaigns and active initiatives, we strive to inspire change and foster collective efforts for a sustainable and protected planet, ensuring a healthier environment for all.
