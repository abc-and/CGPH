Our Cause

Code Green PH is a youth-led organization in the Philippines, founded on April 14, 2022, with the mission of promoting environmental sustainability and conservation. Our cause is driven by the urgent need to address the environmental crisis through collective action and solidarity. Together, we strive for a sustainable future, where every action counts toward preserving the environment for future generations.

Our Mission and Vision

Mission

From the beginning, our mission has been to promote environmental sustainability and conservation through various actions and mass information dissemination regarding the current situation of our planet. Today, many people across the globe are clamoring for climate justice, and we, the Code Green PH, felt the urge to move and also do something. We consider this as a privilege to be able to help save our planet and partake in making a safer and well-protected environment to live in.

Vision

We aim to have a healthy and well-protected environment that supports a sustainable society. We want a green legacy that would ignite hope and push people of tomorrow to move and continue to contribute something beneficial for our planet.
As an environmental-centered organization, the Code Green PH would continuously improve itself to have a better and faster response to environmental crises.

Our Goals and Objectives

To initiate environmental actions within communities.

To disseminate information regarding the current state of our environment.

To nurture children and teach them what they can do, in their own little ways, to help save and protect the planet.
